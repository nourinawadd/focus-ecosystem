How Anchor works

Key benefits for your focus efficiency.

Anchor closes the gap between intending to focus and actually doing it — with a physical start ritual, OS-level enforcement, and a score you can trust.

Tap to start — no tap, no session

Touch your NFC tag on your desk or keychain. The physical act is the deliberate ritual that opens a session and starts the clock.

Distractions gone at the OS level

Via Apple's Family Controls, blocking isn't a suggestion — it's enforced at the system level for the duration of your session.

A score computed on the server

Your focus score is derived from authoritative session data, not what you report. It can't be inflated or faked.

About Anchor

Focus you can actually trust.

Anchor began with a small frustration. Most focus apps ask you to be honest about the time you lose to distraction, then happily let you mark a session "complete" while you scroll somewhere else. When focus is self-reported, the numbers stop meaning anything.

We wanted focus you could actually prove. So we built Anchor around a real, physical moment. You tap an NFC tag to begin, your distracting apps switch off at the system level, and your score is worked out on our servers from what genuinely happened during the session. No tap, no session.

Anchor grew out of a graduation project and a real belief that good habits deserve real tools. It stays small, independent, and built by people who rely on it every day to get their own work done.

Proof over promises

A session only counts when you physically show up for it, so your stats reflect what you really did, not what you meant to do.

Calm by design

No guilt trips and no noisy gamification. Just clear, quiet signals about when and how you focus best.

Your data, respected

We never sell your information and never run ads. Your app-blocking choices stay private on your device.
