How Anchor works

Key benefits for your focus efficiency.

Anchor closes the gap between intending to focus and actually doing it — with a physical start ritual, OS-level enforcement, and a score you can trust.

Tap to start — no tap, no session

Touch your NFC tag on your desk or keychain. The physical act is the deliberate ritual that opens a session and starts the clock.

Distractions gone at the OS level

Via Apple's Family Controls, blocking isn't a suggestion — it's enforced at the system level for the duration of your session.

A score computed on the server

Your focus score is derived from authoritative session data, not what you report. It can't be inflated or faked.
